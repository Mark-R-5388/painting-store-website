Creator of AMV Designs

Hello! Welcome to my fluid art webpage! I am a licensed interior designer, artist and fitness enthusiast. I've been an artist since I was a young girl, and have developed a passion for fluid artistry, which is abstract painting. I use my eye for design and presentation to soften and allow my natural creative abilities to flow through the paint mediums. Since I'm always looking to express a wide range of skills, techniques and styles; I'm open to exploring and growing as an artist - therefore I will continue to develop my craft. I hope you enjoy my work! Please contact me for artwork you see on here, as well as, commissioned pieces. Everything you see is original. No two paintings are the same. If the piece you see is no longer available; I can use the same colors and technique to create a similar vibe and effect. Since all paintings are abstract your painting will be like no other! Thank you for taking a look at my designs and please spread this along to your friends and family!
Have a blessed day,
Alexandria
